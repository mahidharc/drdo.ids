Working Visualizations of the NBC

The following figure shows how the NBC works. The NBC works on a Priori and Posteriori learning model. Its output is the Maximum of the Posteriori or MAP. A priori learning is knowledge known without experience. A posteriori learning is knowledge known with experience. As you can see in the illustration below, knowledge gained through experience is constantly changing. So taking into account the Priori and Posteriori learning, the likelihood of the given data Y is calculated.
